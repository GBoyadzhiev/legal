# Support

## Contact Support

If you have questions, feedback, bug reports, or need assistance with Litsi, please contact:

**Support Email**

[gbindie.dev+support@gmail.com](mailto:gbindie.dev+support@gmail.com)

## What to Include

When reporting a problem, it helps to include:

* Device model
* iOS version
* Litsi version
* Description of the issue
* Steps to reproduce the problem
* Screenshots if applicable

## Subscriptions and Billing

Subscriptions are managed by Apple through the App Store.

For issues related to:

* Charges
* Refund requests
* Payment methods
* Subscription cancellations

please use Apple's subscription management tools and support channels.

## Privacy

Litsi is designed with a privacy-first approach.

Your journal entries remain stored locally on your device.

Litsi does not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or journal content.

For privacy-related questions, please contact:

[gbindie.dev+support@gmail.com](mailto:gbindie.dev+support@gmail.com)

## Feedback

Feature requests and product feedback are always welcome.

Litsi is actively developed and user feedback helps shape future improvements.
