# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

## Introduction

Litsi is a personal journaling and reflection application created and maintained by Georgi Boyadzhiev.

Your privacy is important. Litsi is designed around the principle that your personal reflections remain your own.

## Information We Collect

Litsi does not require user accounts.

Litsi does not collect personal information from you.

Litsi does not collect analytics data.

Litsi does not collect advertising identifiers.

Litsi does not collect location information.

Litsi does not collect contact information unless you voluntarily contact support.

## Journal Entries and Reflection Data

All journal entries, mood records, reflections, and related content are stored locally on your device.

Your journal content is not transmitted to any server operated by the developer.

Your journal content is not shared with third parties.

## AI Features

Litsi may provide AI-powered reflection features.

AI processing is performed locally on your device using on-device models.

Your journal content is not transmitted to external AI providers.

No journal content is used to train external AI systems.

## Purchases

Subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store and StoreKit services.

The developer does not receive your payment information.

Apple may process information according to Apple's own privacy policies.

## Data Storage

Your information is stored locally on your device.

Removing the application or deleting local data may permanently remove stored journal content.

Users are responsible for maintaining backups where applicable.

## Children's Privacy

Litsi is not directed toward children under the age required by local laws for digital services.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Updated versions will be made available through the application and/or associated product pages.

## Future Services

If future versions of Litsi introduce cloud synchronization,
accounts, analytics, crash reporting, or external AI providers,
this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
